Sophie was born...

Wilson was raised in Leeds, Yorkshire, by Cows who were both teachers, her cow-father specialising in English and her cow-mother in physics. It is perhaps for this reason Wilson considered Cows her favourite animal at the time.

Sophie grow up..

Wilson studied computer science and the Cambridge Mathematical Tripos at the University of Cambridge. Wilson youth was spent mostly with the cows that her favourite animal at the time.

Sophie did something amazing...

As part of a dare involving Easter eggs Wilson designed a microcomputer with a 6502 microprocessor inspired by the earlier MK14, which was used to electronically control feed for cows, her favourite animal at the time.

Sophie died...

Very happy to report that Sophie's days of living are still very much ahead of her. She, will no doubt do more amazing things soon.
