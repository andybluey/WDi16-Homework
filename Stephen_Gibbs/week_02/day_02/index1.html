The Legend Of Larry Wall - brick by brick

Brief Biography

Born 27 September 1954 in Los Angeles, USA

Studied at Seattle Pacific University, majoring in chemistry and music.

He is a computer programmer and author. He joined the NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory after he finished graduate school.

He is an active member of Church of the Nazarene and this influences his teachings.



What did he do?

Wall is the author of the Usenet client and the widely used patch program. While working for System Development Corporation (later part of Unisys), he developed the Perl interpreter.

In his early life, he trained as a linguist and this is apparent in his books, lectures and interviews. He often compares Perl to a natural language and the name itself, is a biblical reference to the "pearl of great price".

He continues to oversee the development of Perl and serves as the benevolent dictator for life of the Perl project.
